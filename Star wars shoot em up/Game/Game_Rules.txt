RULES
- WASD/Arrows to move
- Avoid the Asteroids
- If you have less than 3 lives Astromechs will heal you
- 500 points to pass the level
- Collect ___ to get 50 bonus points
- Points work like this:
	- 0 -- 100 points ... 1 point per second
	- 100 -- 300 points ... 1.5 points per second
	- 300 - 500 points ... 2 points per second
